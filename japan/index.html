Japan

Japan was never on my radar until I was convinced to come along with some friends on a trip there. After the trip, I thought about Japan constantly. I kept finding myself getting pulled back to the country. I kept booking trips, hoping that my curiosity would finally be satisfied, but I seem to never reach that point. I have even been lucky enough to spent a few extended periods there, and have found myself sometimes loving the country and sometimes hating it.

In any case, it's undeniably a great place for photographers and tourists in general.

I have so much to say, but I had better let the photos do some of the talking.
